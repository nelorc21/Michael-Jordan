Overall Life:

Michael Jeffery Jordan or as most know him MJ is the greatest basketball player in the History of the NBA. His proffesional basketball career is certainly one to remember. He went through many hardships throughout his life to be able to obtain this title. From being doubted and rejeccted in the begining of his carrer, to experiencing the "Jordan Rule" in the NBA to losing his dad Jordan's determination and perserverience helped him overcome all these obstacles. Fighting through all these hardships and continuing to perfect his craft is what led him to be a common name in every dicussion/debate of basketball. Between his basketball careern and his other sources of income (including Jordan Brand) he is defintely one of if not the most succesfful NBA legends ever.

Is Michael Jordan someone you should look up to?

Michael Jordan is a great role model especially for athletes because of how over the year of his career he displays how important it is to perfect your craft. If you want to be the best at something then taking a deep look into Jordan's work ethic is probably your best bet. MJ once said " Ican accept failure, everyone fails at something. But i can't accept not trying." this quote perfectly explains how hard working he was because he never gave up, in every situation he always gave it his best regardless of the outcome. This mindset is a very good one for one to have because as long as you put in all your effort then your hard work will never go unnoticed.
